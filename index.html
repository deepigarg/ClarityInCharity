Benificiary

Store

Setup a shop? A shop receives coins from the donors corresponding to a project's requirements

Join the Clarity in Charity network today!

Become part of the charity network by connecting your Metamask wallet. Our platform acts as a bridge between organisations which require supplies and donors who are ready to donate in ethers. The cooperative shops are like normal shops but they accept payment in cryptocurrency.

  • Add project acording to your needs.
  • Donate hasslefree using Metamask wallet.
  • Initiate a transaction. The project signs transaction when they need the money. Approve money transfer and make world a better place

No account creation required to donate. Just connect the wallet and choose a project you would like to help. View project details to know where the project spends money. Transfer is directly to shops so your money is not misused.
